# Draft Deposit Description

This deposit contains the reproducibility materials supporting the manuscript `Beyond-AHI hypoxic metrics and hard cardiovascular and mortality outcomes in adult OSA-related cohorts: a systematic review and meta-analysis`.

Included materials cover the executed search and protocol appendix, title/abstract screening and full-text review logs, the final cohort-level extraction master, final risk-of-bias source table, final synthesis TSV backbones, AF harmonization support files, the final export and figure-generation script, and the final upload-ready figure PDFs plus key supplementary CSV files.

The package documents two linked layers:

- the historical executed package: `24` unique articles and `45` cohort-level rows
- the updated final submission dataset: `31` unique articles and `54` cohort-level rows

The repository also documents that a final anchor-centered citation-chasing completeness pass was completed after the post-freeze evidence-upgrade supplement and did not identify any additional protocol-concordant retained study beyond the integrated `31 / 54` dataset.

This deposit is intended as a transparency and reproducibility resource. It does not redistribute copyrighted publisher PDFs.
